   remove_endings(line, endings)

      Remove from the line any trailing ignore markers.

      For each ending provided, this function uses a regex pattern to
      match the literal ending, optionally followed by a colon and
      error codes, and any extra whitespace until the end of the line.
      It repeatedly removes the matched pattern for each ending.
